In 2024, the women's NCAA tournament game brought in more viewership than the men's game. This mark has not been broken again since, but millions of viewers are still tuned in to women's March Madness. Curiously, you might notice that "March Madness", no prefix, automatically applies to the men, while the women's game must always be prefaced. Frankly, I am not interested by the men's tournament. So here's a website entirely dedicated to the true March Madness.
